> Cognised existence: a managed aquaculture production site and its associated monitoring context, including fish and shellfish operations and related intake, outlet, and discharge information.

## Core Question

Where are aquaculture activities located, and what production or discharge characteristics are reported there?

## Scope

- Fish and shellfish aquaculture production locations
- Aquaculture facility intake and outlet monitoring points
- Site-level status, measurement, and discharge-linked records
