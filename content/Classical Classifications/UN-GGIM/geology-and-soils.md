> **UN-GGIM Definition:** Geological formations, soil types, and subsurface characteristics.

## SPHERE Mapping

A **thread theme** bridging Lithosphere (bedrock geology, subsurface) and Pedosphere (soil classification, properties). UN-GGIM uniquely groups these together, recognising that geology and soils are a continuum from deep rock to surface soil. See [[Classical Classifications/INSPIRE/geology|INSPIRE Geology]] and [[Classical Classifications/INSPIRE/soil|INSPIRE Soil]].
