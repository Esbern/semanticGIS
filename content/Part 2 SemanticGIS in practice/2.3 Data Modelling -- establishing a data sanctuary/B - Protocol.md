- **Inventory Wild Data:** List all potential sources (CVR, DAR, Field Notes).
    
- **Apply the Evaluation Matrix:**
    
    - **Accept** data that matches your NOIR attributes.
        
    - **Transform** fragmented data through joins and schema mapping.
        
    - **Reject** data that fails your intent, leading to **Fieldwork** or **Backflow**.
        
- **Execute Sanitisation Rituals:** Perform the technical "Spade Work" (Joins/Geocoding/Overlays).
    
- **Procure Primary Data:** Conduct fieldwork to capture missing phenomena (e.g., soundboxes, urban design).
    
- **Manifest the Sanctuary:** Document each dataset's "Grounding Biography" in an `.md` manifest.
