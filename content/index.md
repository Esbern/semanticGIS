Welcome to SemanticGIS.ORG. This site presents international and global geospatial datasets organised according to the principles of SemanticGIS — a semantic framework for reasoning about spatial data across sources, scales, and domains.

For Danish national data, see [semanticgis.dk](https://semanticgis.dk/).

## How to Navigate Data

### 1. [[SPHERE|By Sphere (Semantic Domains)]]

Navigate by thematic interest. Spheres organise the world into domains — Socio-Technical, Atmosphere, Biosphere, Hydrosphere, Lithosphere, Pedosphere, Toposphere — each decomposed into leaves that answer specific spatial questions.

- Best if you are asking a research question (e.g., "Where are buildings?", "What is the elevation?", "Where are protected habitats?")
- Each leaf links to the international datasets that realise it

### 2. [[Datasets by Collection|By Collection]]

Browse by dataset programme or collection — OSM, Copernicus, Natural Earth, Overture Maps, and others.

### 3. [[Data Portals|By Data Portal]]

Understand how to access data from each international source: query patterns, auth, service types.
