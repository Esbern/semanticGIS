> **INSPIRE Definition:** Location and operation of environmental monitoring facilities, including observation and measurement of emissions and the state of environmental media.

## SPHERE Mapping

A major **thread theme** — the monitoring *facilities* are human services (Socio-Technical). What they *observe* spans all physical spheres: air quality stations (Atmosphere), water quality sensors (Hydrosphere), soil sampling sites (Pedosphere), ecosystem plots (Biosphere), and geological boreholes (Lithosphere).
