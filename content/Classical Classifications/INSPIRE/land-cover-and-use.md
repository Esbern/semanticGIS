> **INSPIRE Definition:** Physical and biological cover of the Earth's surface including artificial surfaces, agricultural areas, forests, and water bodies. Territory characterised according to its current and future planned functional dimension.

## SPHERE Mapping

A major **thread theme** spanning the entire SPHERE tree. Land *cover* links to Biosphere (vegetation), Hydrosphere (water bodies), and Pedosphere (bare soil). Land *use* is inherently Socio-Technical (zoning, planning). The interpreted thematic product lives in Socio-Technical Perception & Thematics, while raw evidence stays in Toposphere imagery.
