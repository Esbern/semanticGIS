Human thematic partitioning of space. This twig represents how humans classify, symbolize, and communicate territory through thematic and topographic interpretation products.

## Scope

- Thematic map products derived from imagery, surveys, and administrative interpretation
- Historical and modern topographic map representations
- Human zoning and categorization overlays used for planning and communication
- Cartographic partitioning where the cognitive framing is part of the data meaning

## Distinction

- Raw observational image evidence belongs in [[SPHERE/Toposphere/RS/index|toposphere_remote_sensed_observations]].
- Physical surface geometry belongs in [[SPHERE/Toposphere/Topography/index|toposphere_topography_bathymetry]].
- This twig stores the interpreted thematic framing used by humans.
