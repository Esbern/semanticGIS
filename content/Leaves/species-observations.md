> Cognised existence: a species occurrence record tied to a location and observation time, documenting biodiversity evidence in the landscape or water environment.

## Core Question

Which species have been observed at this location, and when were they recorded?

## Scope

- Point or area-based species observation records
- Historical and recent biodiversity observations
- Taxon-level occurrence and observer-linked metadata
- Observation exchanges and standards such as Darwin Core
- Regulatory-focused subsets such as Habitatdirektivets bilag IV observations

## Typical Realisations

- Alle fund fra Arter.dk
- Alle fund fra Arter.dk, historiske
- Arter Resultat
- Artsfund i Naturdatabasen (Darwin Core)
- Fund af arter på Habitatdirektivets bilag IV fra Arter.dk

## Interpretation Note

This leaf models observation evidence, not management units. It complements ecosystem, fauna, and habitat interpretations by providing event-level occurrences.
