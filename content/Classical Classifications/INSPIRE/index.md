The INSPIRE Directive (2007/2/EC) defines 34 spatial data themes across three Annexes, mandating interoperability for European spatial data infrastructure. These themes are legally binding for EU/EEA Member States and are the primary classification used in European metadata catalogs.

Each theme below is mapped to its location in the [[SPHERE]] tree. INSPIRE themes are more granular than ISO 19115 but still flat — themes like "Environmental Monitoring Facilities" or "Natural Risk Zones" inherently span multiple spheres.

**Directive:** INSPIRE Directive 2007/2/EC, Annexes I–III
