> Cognised existence: a legally defined planning or zoning unit that governs where and how activities or development are allowed.

## Core Question

Which planning or zoning decisions apply here, and what constraints do they impose?

## Scope

- Fingerplan and related regional planning delineations
- Landzone decisions and planning-law based restrictions
- Adopted and proposed building-field planning units

## Typical Realisations

- Afgrænsning af CPH Roskilde (Fingerplan)
- Beslutning om tilbageførsel til landzone
- Byggefelt, vedtaget
