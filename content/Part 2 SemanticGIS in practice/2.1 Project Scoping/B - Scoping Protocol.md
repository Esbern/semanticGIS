 # The Scoping Protocol 

_Follow these steps to complete Phase 1 of your SemanticGIS Project._

### 1. Deconstructing the Brief

- **1.1 Core Questions:** Translate the client's "Wild Intent" into a grounded geospatial question. Identify your primary and proxy entities.
    
- **1.2 Epistemological Nature:** Is this project **Heuristic** (discovery) or **Teleological** (goal-oriented)? Decide if you need to reverse-engineer the workflow.
    
- **1.3 Omissions & Bias:** Identify the "Invisible City" (harbours, parks, food trucks) that your official data might miss.
    

### 2. Mapping the Operational Context

- **2.1 Stakeholders:** Define the Human Ontology (Client, Gatekeeper, End-User).
    
- **2.2 The Tech Stack:** List mandated software and environment constraints.
    
- **2.3 Data Delivery:** Document what the client is providing and in what format.
    
- **2.4 Business Rules & Constraints:** Explicitly list any "Banned" sources or mandated methodologies required by the client’s legal or IT departments.
    
- **2.5 Governance & Ethics:** Define your GDPR and privacy strategy.
