> Cognised existence: a delimited groundwater body, aquifer, or groundwater management unit used to describe subsurface water occurrence and condition.

## Core Question

Which groundwater body or aquifer is present here, and what status applies?

## Scope

- Terrænnære, regionale, and dybe groundwater bodies
- Aquifers and drinking-water abstractions as mapped management units
- Groundwater chemistry and status layers attached to those bodies
- Protection and planning zones specifically tied to groundwater systems

## Typical Realisations

- VP3 - Afgrænsning. Grundvand, terrænnære forekomster
- VP3 - Afgrænsning. Grundvand, regionale forekomster
- VP3 - Afgrænsning. Grundvand, dybe forekomster

## Interpretation Note

This leaf is about the groundwater body as a hydrological unit. Site-specific contamination or exposure assessments may also connect to contaminant and risk threads, but the groundwater unit itself belongs here.
