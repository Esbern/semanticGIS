> Cognised existence: ecological condition state of a habitat area, represented through measured or derived quality indicators.

## Core Question

What ecological condition or quality is reported for the habitat at this location?

## Scope

- Habitat quality and naturtilstand indicators
- Condition and biodiversity scoring layers tied to habitat units
- Ecological status interpretation across mapped habitat systems
- HNV indicators, value classes, distribution classes, and forest proxy layers used as habitat-quality signals
- Regulatory habitat sensitivity categories and forest continuity/value proxies used to assess ecological sensitivity

## Typical Realisations

- NP3 - Lysåbne naturtyper (2016-2019) - Naturtilstand
- Biodiversitetskortet - Habitatnatur
- Biodiversitetskortet - Bioscore
- HNV national indicator layers and HNV skov indicators
- Kategori 1-3 natur layers and ammoniakfølsom skov proxy layers
