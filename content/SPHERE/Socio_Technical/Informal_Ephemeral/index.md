Informal, tactical, and short-lived human systems that are weakly represented in official registries.

## Scope

- Tactical urbanism and temporary installations
- Informal service and mobility patterns
- Short-lived event infrastructure and ad hoc land occupation
- Unregistered socio-technical behaviors that still affect spatial analysis

This twig is essential for capturing human systems that are real in practice but under-specified in formal cadastres and long-cycle registries.
