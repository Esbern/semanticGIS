> Cognised existence: fisheries activity intensity represented as spatial pressure signals from AIS and derived effort analyses.

## Core Question

What fishing effort and gear activity occur in this marine area, and how does it relate to ecosystem pressure and regulation?

## Scope

- AIS-derived fishing gear activity distributions
- Aggregated fishing intensity products used in planning cycles
- Marine effort signals supporting ecosystem pressure interpretation and governance controls

## Typical Realisations

- VP3 - Analyseret fiskeriintensitet 2013-2018
- NP3 - AIS Garn
- NP3 - AIS Pelagiske redskaber

## Threading Rationale

This leaf is anchored in resource utilization but intentionally threaded to governance (rules and designations) and ecosystems (pressure on habitats and species).
